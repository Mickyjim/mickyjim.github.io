Project 1
Tutifruti

Tutifruiti is an app that let's you discover the virtues of certain fruits and dried fruits that you find in the fruit market and/or in certain places in the world. Especially that some are uncommon and intriguing. Each fruit is accompanied by a full description of it's origins, health benefits and a detailed nutritional value per 100g of vitamins, minerals, calories, sugar, fat and proteins. This app gives you knowledge of what you eat in times of great questions about what we consume on a day to day basis.
